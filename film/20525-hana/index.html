花よりもなほ

Hana

2006 · 監督: 是枝裕和 · 128分 · アクション / ドラマ / 履歴 / コメディ

元禄15年。父親の仇討ちという宿命を背負って信州から江戸に赴き、貧乏長屋に身を潜めた若侍（岡田）。長屋の人々と触れ合ううち、彼の決心が揺らいでいくさまを描く。物語の背景に赤穂浪士の討ち入りを盛り込みつつ、逆手にとって、「弱いものが弱いまま肯定される物語を目指した」（是枝）。これまでフィクションとノンフィクションの距離を意識して劇映画を撮ってきた是枝が、初めてどっぷりと物語世界に挑んだ意欲作。

In the 15th year of the Genroku era. A young samurai (Okada) burdened by the duty to avenge his father travels from Shinshu to Edo and hides out in a tenement slum. The film depicts how his resolve begins to waver as he interacts with the residents of the tenement. While weaving the story of the Ako Ronin's raid into the background, director Kore-eda turns it on its head, aiming for 'a story where the weak are affirmed as they are.' This ambitious work marks Kore-eda's first deep dive into a narrative world, having previously been conscious of the distance between fiction and nonfiction in his live-action films.
