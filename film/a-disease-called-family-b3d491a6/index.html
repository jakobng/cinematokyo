家族という病

A Disease Called Family

2026 · 監督: 大山千賀子 · 96分

作家・下重暁子が2015年に刊行し、「家族信仰」を問いただす内容が話題を集めて60万部のベストセラーとなったエッセイを、「僕の名前はルシアン」の大山千賀子が脚本・監督を兼ねて映画化。普通であることにしがみつく人々の滑稽さと狂気を、ブラックユーモアを交えて描いた群像劇。 売れっ子のミステリー小説家・田原純一郎は、強烈な妻・麻里子に人生を支配され、本来の才能を眠らせたままでいる。定年後も母の期待を裏切れず、引きこもりの息子を抱えながら、偽りの重役を演じ続ける元エリートサラリーマン・松原雄二。母がAV女優だという噂が学校中に広がり、追い詰められていく女子高生・黒木まりあ。そんな3つの家族の前に現れたのは、裸体をテーマに文学賞を受賞した新鋭作家・藤田森だった。さらに、公園で自由気ままに暮らすホームレスの女性も、彼らとはまったく異なる存在として現れ……。 出演は藤本隆宏、友近、渡辺えり、小出恵介、寺泉憲、榊原愛、廣井若菜ら。

Writer Shimojo Akiko's 2015 essay, which questioned the concept of 'family worship' and became a bestseller with 600,000 copies, is adapted into a film by director-screenwriter Oyama Chikako of 'My Name is Lucia.' This ensemble piece, laced with black humor, depicts the absurdity and madness of people clinging to normalcy. Bestselling mystery novelist Tahara Junichiro has his life controlled by his intense wife Mariko, leaving his true talent dormant. Former elite salaryman Matsubara Yuji, unable to betray his mother's expectations even after retirement, continues to play the role of a fake executive while dealing with his hikikomori son. High school girl Kuroki Maria is driven into a corner when rumors spread that her mother is an AV actress. Appearing before these three families is Fujita Shin, an up-and-coming writer who won a literary award for a work themed around nudity. Additionally, a homeless woman living freely in the park appears as a completely different presence. Starring Fujimoto Takahiro, Tomochika, Watanabe Eri, Koide Keisuke, Teraishi Ken, Sakakibara Ai, and Hiroi Wakana.
