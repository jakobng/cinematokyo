1972 · 監督: アンドレイ・タルコフスキー · 165分 · ドラマ / サイエンスフィクション / 謎

When communication is lost with the space station Prometheus, which is exploring the ocean-and-cloud-covered planet Solaris, psychologist Kris is sent to investigate. Upon arriving at the Prometheus, Kris witnesses the suicide of a friend, traces of people who shouldn't be there, and a series of inexplicable phenomena caused by the sentient organic ocean.
