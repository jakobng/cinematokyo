白夜

Four Nights of a Dreamer

1972 · 監督: ロベール・ブレッソン · 82分 · ドラマ / ロマンス

Jacques, an aspiring painter, saves a young woman named Marthe from throwing herself into the Seine one night. She had a lover who left for America a year ago, and that very night was when they had promised to reunite. Jacques finds her, sunk in anguish, beautiful. Driven by a fierce impulse of love, he forces himself to remain calm, hoping her heart will drift away from her lover, while feeling guilt over this. Like the 1957 version, this is a modern adaptation of Dostoevsky's famous tragic love story, a poignant work revealing Bresson's awakened worldview. Visconti's romanticism is absent here; the young man's blind love is brutally exposed as he pours out his feelings into a mini tape recorder, a habit of his, making it painful to watch.
