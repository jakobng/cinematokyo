お笑えない芸人

Chuckle Chuckle

2026 · 監督: 西田祐香 · 76分 · コメディ / ファンタジー / ドラマ

夢をあきらめかけた男が、目の前に現れた“理想の自分”との奇妙な共同生活を送りながら、夢と現実、理想と本音のはざまでもがく姿をユーモラスに描いたドラマ。監督の西田祐香が、京都芸術大学映画学科の卒業制作作品として手がけた。 芸人を目指す佐原は、相方の瀬戸口と「激甘酢豚」というコンビで活動してきた。しかし、ある出来事をきっかけにコンビは解散し、佐原は夢半ばで立ち止まってしまう。そんな佐原の前に、自分に瓜二つのサハラという男が現れる。サハラは芸人として爆発的に成功した、佐原が思い描いた理想の自分だった。分身であるサハラは佐原に成功のノウハウを語り、佐原の人生に大きく介入し始める。 「恋も噂も七十五日」の吉野真生が佐原とサハラの二役を演じ、「ハローマイフレンド」の村山暁が相方の瀬戸口役を演じる。若手映像作家の登竜門であるSKIPシティ国際Dシネマ映画祭2025でスペシャル・メンションを授与された。

A fusion of individuality from promising young creators. Directed by Yuka Nishida, a graduate of Kyoto University of the Arts, who now belongs to STARDUST CREATORS as a screenwriter. This film is her graduation project, made when she was 22. It has been nominated for the SKIP City International D-Cinema Festival 2025, the 23rd Ueda Castle Town Film Festival Independent Film Competition, and the 28th Kyoto International Student Film Festival. Lead actor Mao Yoshino passionately plays dual roles: Ichitaro Sahara and his 'ideal self' Sahara. After this film, Yoshino was cast as a main host in 'Love and Rumors Last Seventy-Five Days' (2023, directed by Ririko Shinjo). Satoru Murayama, playing Junji Setoguchi who forms a duo 'Geki Amazu Buta' with Yoshino, portrays his partner while carrying a difficult-to-share secret, and was selected as a main cast member from about 700 applicants for the 2026 film 'Hello My Friend' (2026, directed by Masahide Ichii). Nanami Kitano, playing Sahara's neighbor Suzuka Murii, expresses a delicate distance with Sahara through a transparent performance, and has appeared in many films since. Distribution is handled by GeneHeart, which operates 'GeneTheater,' a streaming service curating only high-quality Japanese short films that stir emotions.
