シンシン アンド ザ マウス

Sinsin and the Mouse

2026 · 監督: 真壁幸紀 · 108分 · ロマンス

母を亡くし、深い喪失感を抱えたまま日々を過ごす、ちづみ。心の空白は埋まらず、時間だけが過ぎていくなか、友人に誘われ、台湾を訪れた。そこで、台湾人の母と日本人の父を持つ・シンシンを紹介される。見知らぬ街の風景と、何気ない会話の積み重ねが、止まっていた心を少しずつ動かしていく。消えない悲しみを抱えながらも、小さなぬくもりを見つけていく――。

A human drama based on a story from Banana Yoshimoto's short story collection 'Mitten and Fubin', co-produced by Japan and Taiwan. It quietly depicts a woman carrying loss who, through encounters in a foreign land, gradually finds renewal. Chizumi is a woman living each day with deep grief after losing her mother. As time passes without filling the void in her heart, she is invited by a friend to visit Taiwan. There, she meets Shin-shin, a young man with a Taiwanese mother and a Japanese father. The unfamiliar cityscapes and the accumulation of casual conversations slowly begin to move her frozen heart, and Chizumi, while still carrying her lingering sense of loss, discovers small warmth. Yukino Kishii ('Small, Slow but Steady', 'Just the Two of Us') plays the protagonist Chizumi, and popular young Taiwanese actor Tseng Jing-hua (known for 'The Soul of the Prisoner', 'Your Name Engraved Herein') plays Shin-shin, the young man she meets in Taiwan. The cast also includes Tokio Fujiwara, Seina Nakata, Tokio Emoto, Kayo Ise, Kisuke Iida, and Kimiko Yo. Directed and scripted by Yukinori Makabe ('I Am a Monk.', 'Scoop!').
