Digimon Adventure

1999 · 監督: 細田守 · 20分 · アニメーション / アドベンチャー / アクション / ファンタジー / ファミリー

The first theatrical film in the "Digimon" series. From a midnight computer screen, a strange creature named "Botamon" is born! While Tai is uneasy, his little sister Kari is overjoyed. Botamon evolves into Agumon! Suddenly, the Digital World, home to digital monsters, appears. A bird-like monster charges at Agumon. But then, another change occurs in Agumon's body... Evolution into the giant monster "Greymon" begins! Two colossal monsters clash—what will be the outcome of this fierce battle?
