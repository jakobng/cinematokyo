Pickpocket

1959 · 監督: ロベール・ブレッソン · 75分 · 犯罪 / ドラマ

Michel, a poor university student, discovers by chance that he has a talent for pickpocketing. Though once caught by a detective, he is released due to lack of evidence and soon targets others' wallets again. Eventually, his methods become organized as he brings in accomplices. In 'A Man Escaped,' Bresson realistically and thrillingly depicted a prison break; here, he turns his focus to pickpocketing, introducing actual techniques while portraying a lonely young man distrustful of others. A gripping masterpiece where the realism born from amateur actors and the suspense created by various pickpocketing scenes blend seamlessly.
