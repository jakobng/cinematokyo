骨までしゃぶる

Nothing But Bones

1966 · 監督: 加藤泰 · 88分 · ドラマ

かいせつ 明治後期の洲崎遊廓に貧農の娘が娼妓として売られ…。骨の髄まで搾取される女たちの悲哀と逞しさを描く、加藤泰流の激烈な傑作女性映画。

In the late Meiji period, a poor farmer's daughter is sold as a prostitute to the Suzaki pleasure quarters. A fierce masterpiece of women's cinema by Tai Kato, depicting the sorrow and resilience of women exploited to the bone.
