二代目はクリスチャン

The Second Is a Christian

1985 · 監督: 井筒和幸 · 101分 · コメディ / 犯罪

Kyoko, a nun at St. Saffron Church on Mount Rokko, plays hymns on the organ. Haruhiko Tenryu, the second-generation head of the Tenryu-gumi yakuza clan, is deeply in love with Kyoko because of her refined beauty, and has even forced his underlings to be baptized as Christians. Also harboring feelings for Kyoko is Detective Jindai, whose family runs a Tendai sect temple, presenting another formidable obstacle. However, Kyoko herself secretly harbors feelings for Eiji, whom she met fatefully one stormy day. As the feelings of those around her intertwine, Kyoko's destiny begins to move in an unexpected direction.
