Room for Let

1959 · 監督: 川島雄三 · 112分 · コメディ

かいせつ 大阪の通天閣を望む広いボロ屋敷には風変りな間借り人たちがいた…。川島との名コンビのフランキーが、代筆やこんにゃく製造などに精を出すなんでも屋を飄々と演じ、浪花、小沢ら個性的な脇役陣もクセになる傑作群像喜劇。

In a large rundown house overlooking the Tsutenkaku Tower in Osaka, a group of eccentric lodgers live... Frankie, in his famous partnership with Kawashima, plays a laid-back jack-of-all-trades who works as a ghostwriter and konnyaku maker. With unique supporting actors like Naniwa and Ozawa, this is a brilliant ensemble comedy that leaves a lasting impression.
