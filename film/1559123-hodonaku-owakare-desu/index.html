ほどなく、お別れです

Hodonaku, Owakare Desu

2026 · 監督: 三木孝浩 · 124分 · ドラマ

Based on the bestselling novel of the same name by Amone Nagatsuki, winner of the Shogakukan Bunko Novel Award Grand Prize, starring Minami Hamabe and Ren Meguro, directed by Takahiro Miki ("Ao Haru Ride," "Tomorrow I Will Date Yesterday's You"). It depicts a heroine who joins a funeral company and, together with her supervising funeral planner, aims to create the "best possible funeral." Misu Shimizu, struggling with job hunting, has a secret she can't tell anyone: she can hear the voices of the deceased. A fateful encounter changes her life when Reiji Urushibara, a funeral planner who notices her ability, invites her into the world of funeral planning. Guided by something, Misu becomes an intern at the funeral company "Bando Kaikan." Teaming up with Urushibara, she confronts the question "What is a funeral that satisfies not only the surviving family but also the deceased?" through various families' funerals, including a husband who lost his pregnant wife and a couple who lost their young daughter. Eventually, Misu comes to admire Urushibara's sincere dedication to the deceased and their families, and decides to pursue a career as a funeral planner herself. Hamabe plays Misu, Meguro plays Urushibara, with a stellar cast including Mochika Yamada, Ken Mitsuishi, Mirai Shida, Keisuke Watanabe, Kotone Furukawa, and Takumi Kitamura. The script is by Takuro Honda (drama "Lion's Snack"), supervised by Yoshikazu Okada, known for various hit works.
