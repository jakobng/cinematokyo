『倖せは俺等のねがい』 ＊デジタル上映

1957 · 監督: 宇野重吉 · 82分

かいせつ 念願の新居に引っ越した結婚目前の工場勤めの二人が、遠縁の四人の子供を押しつけられて…。庶民のささやかな幸せをみつめた名優・宇野の監督第三作。フランキーは本作と『幕末太陽傳』でブルーリボン主演男優賞を受賞。

A couple working at a factory, on the verge of marriage and moving into their long-awaited new home, are saddled with four distant relatives' children... The third directorial work by the renowned actor Uno, who captures the modest happiness of ordinary people. Frankie won the Blue Ribbon Award for Best Actor for this film and 'The Sun of the Meiji Restoration'.
