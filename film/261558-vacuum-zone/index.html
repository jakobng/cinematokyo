真空地帯

Vacuum Zone

1952 · 監督: 山本薩夫 · 129分 · ドラマ

After serving two years in the army prison for stealing an officer's wallet, Kitani returns to his unit. Inside the unit, the squad leader and senior soldiers bully the new recruits. In particular, Private First Class Aita, a university graduate, is subjected to daily violence. Kitani, however, refuses to take part in the bullying. Aita grows fond of Kitani and they become close, but he learns that Kitani has been selected for frontline deployment. Both Kitani's imprisonment and his selection for the front lines were the result of factional infighting.
