The fifth installment in Disney and Pixar's beloved 'Toy Story' series, which has always explored the bond between toys and children. It depicts Woody and Buzz joining forces once again to face an unprecedented threat from modern technology. Buzz, Jessie, Forky, and the others living with Bonnie are enjoying their usual daily life. However, everything changes when the latest electronic tablet, the 'Lilypad,' arrives. Seeing Bonnie absorbed in the multi-functional device, the toys begin to doubt their own purpose. As anxiety spreads that 'toys are no longer needed,' Woody receives an SOS from his friends and returns to Bonnie's side, reuniting with Buzz. The legendary duo stands up to a new threat to protect the place of toys. Directed and written by Andrew Stanton, known for 'Finding Nemo' and 'WALL-E,' who has also worked on the 'Toy Story' series as a story and scriptwriter, bringing a new story as a creator who has supported the series. The co-director is Kena Harris, who directed the Pixar short 'Alberto's Letter.' The Japanese dub features the familiar cast including Toshiaki Karasawa as Woody, George Tokoro as Buzz, Yumi Kusaka, and Ryo Ryusei.
