真田風雲録

Sasuke and His Comedians

1963 · 監督: 加藤泰 · 100分 · ファンタジー / アクション / コメディ

かいせつ 戦国乱世末期の真田十勇士の活躍を描いた破天荒なミュージカル時代劇。時代劇の枠を超えて、型破りの青春群像が熱く綴られる異色の一作。

Director Takashi Kato presents an unconventional period action film depicting the story of the Sanada Ten Braves, who fought in the Winter and Summer Sieges of Osaka, with fantastical fun and excitement! Affected by the radiation of a meteorite that fell from space, the Warring States street urchin Sasuke gains mysterious abilities. Growing up, he calls himself Sarutobi Sasuke and, along with the flying squirrel Okiri, becomes a subordinate of the cool commander Sanada Yukimura, rampaging through the rigged Winter and Summer Sieges of Osaka!
