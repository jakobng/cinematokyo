風たちの午後

Afternoon Breezes

1980 · 監督: 矢崎仁司 · 105分 · ロマンス / ドラマ

Mitsu's birthday. Natsuko buys matching Virgo necklaces and a rose. However, a white handkerchief is hanging in the apartment window. It's the signal that Mitsu's lover, Hideo, is there. Natsuko, who has secretly fallen in love with Mitsu, approaches Hideo in order to have Mitsu all to herself... On a midsummer afternoon, a shocking ending that will shake the world.
