ラプソディ・ラプソディ

Rhapsody, Rhapsody

2026 · 監督: 利重剛 · 106分 · ドラマ

「クロエ」「さよならドビュッシー」などの映画監督作を発表してきた俳優の利重剛が13年ぶりに長編映画のメガホンをとり、主演に高橋一生を迎えて撮りあげた人間ドラマ。人付き合いを避けながら生きてきた男性が、いつの間にか知らない女性に籍を入れられていたことをきっかけに、人生が思わぬ方向へ動き出す様子をユーモラスに描く。 少し天然で絶対に怒らない男・夏野幹夫は、パスポート更新のため戸籍謄本を取得するが、そこに全く身に覚えのない「続柄：妻」の文字を見て驚く。「繁子」という女性が自分と勝手に籍を入れていたことを知った幹夫は、正体不明の彼女を探しはじめる。やがて、街角の小さな花屋で繁子を発見するが、彼女は触れるものすべてを壊してしまう、型破りな女性だった。そんな繁子に振り回される幹夫だったが、奇妙な出会いはいつしかふたりの人生に思いがけない変化をもたらしていく。 心優しく繊細な主人公・幹夫を高橋一生、周囲を翻弄する謎のヒロイン・繁子をNHK連続テレビ小説「まんぷく」の呉城久美が演じ、芹澤興人、池脇千鶴が共演。さらに利重監督もキーパーソンとして自ら出演した。世界的ジャズピアニストの大西順子が音楽を担当。

Actor Tsuyoshi Rishu, who has directed films such as 'Chloe' and 'Goodbye Debussy', takes the director's chair for a feature film for the first time in 13 years, with Issei Takahashi in the lead role. This human drama humorously depicts a man who has avoided social interactions, whose life takes an unexpected turn when he discovers he has been unknowingly registered as married to a woman he doesn't know. Mikio Natsuno, a slightly naive man who never gets angry, goes to get a copy of his family register to renew his passport and is shocked to find the words 'Relationship: Wife' for a woman he has absolutely no memory of. Learning that a woman named 'Shigeko' has registered herself as his wife without his knowledge, Mikio begins searching for this mysterious woman. He eventually finds Shigeko at a small flower shop on a street corner, but she is an unconventional woman who destroys everything she touches. Though Mikio is tossed around by Shigeko, this strange encounter gradually brings unexpected changes to both their lives. Issei Takahashi plays the kind-hearted and sensitive protagonist Mikio, and Kumi Goshima (NHK morning drama 'Manpuku') plays the enigmatic heroine Shigeko who bewilders everyone around her. Okihiko Serizawa and Chizuru Ikewaki co-star, and director Rishu also appears as a key character. World-renowned jazz pianist Junko Onishi provides the music.
